1. PRODUCT VISION
UdaanSetu is an AI-powered digital career & skill mentor specifically built for rural students and youth who have recently completed their schooling or Class 10th. It serves as a comprehensive guidance system to:
- Navigate post-school career confusion in regional languages (Gujarati & English).
- Perform adaptive psychometric and skill-based assessments.
- Conduct deep SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) on student data.
- Generate structured Bio-Profiles identifying core traits and potential.
- Predict career paths using real-time job market data and RAG-driven insights.
- Provide a persistent, context-aware AI Mentor for ongoing conversation and guidance.

3. CORE MODULES
- Auth: Secure Firebase authentication (Google/Email).
- Q&A Engine: Adaptive, logic-driven bilingual questions mapping to professional traits.
- SWOT Engine: Analyzes assessment results to identify Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats.
- Profile Engine: Converts raw assessment data into a refined Student Bio-Profile.
- RAG Engine: Connects the Bio-Profile with a vast database of careers and real-time market data.
- Career Report Generator: Predicts optimal career paths and generates a path-to-success roadmap.
- Mentor Chat: A context-aware companion that remembers the student's report and profile for personalized advice.

4. DESIGN & BRAND IDENTITY
- Mood: Premium, hope-filled, technologically advanced yet culturally rooted.
- Visuals: Sunrise gradients, glassmorphism, and bridge-inspired UI components.
- Languages: Primary focus on native Gujarati support alongside English.
