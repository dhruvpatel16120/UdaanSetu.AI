UDAANSETU.AI — FINAL SYSTEM CONTEXT

Team: FutureMinds
Tagline: “Bridging Rural Youth to Their Future.”

1. PRODUCT VISION

udaansetu.ai is an AI-powered career & skill mentor for rural youth (Class 10+, dropouts, early job seekers), built to:

Remove post-school confusion

Provide personalized, realistic career paths

Work in Gujarati & English

Be ethical, practical, and future-focused

Act as a digital mentor, not just a chatbot

The system combines:

Dynamic psychology-based assessment

Judgement + scoring logic

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

Contextual conversational AI mentor
